General quality analysis
The overall quality of the application can be estimated as Below Medium. A total of 54 defects were reported during testing. All reported defects remain Open and Unresolved. The most significant risks are related to checkout completion, pricing accuracy, gallery navigation and compliance with the available requirements. Release recommendation: Not Recommended. A detailed quality analysis is presented below.
 
Detailed quality analysis
The total number of defects by severity
 
The chart shows the statistics of defects by the degree of the negative impact they have on the application. Out of 54 defects, 3 have Critical severity, 47 have Major severity, and 4 have Minor severity. Major defects prevail and represent about 87% of all reported issues.
 
Test platform
 
Testing was performed on Windows 11 Pro, 64-bit, using Google Chrome 150.0.7871.115, 64-bit. The application under test was GregOlsen.PhotoStudio. The test documentation included the project requirements and available functional documentation. Testing was performed from July 7, 2026 to July 15, 2026.
 
Total defects by module
 
The chart represents all 54 defects reported during testing, grouped by real site modules. The largest groups are Checkout (13), Contact (9), Sessions and Clients Gallery (6 each), and Work (5). Home has no defects assigned directly to its content.

Contact
•  Below Medium
Nine defects affect the contact form validation. Several fields accept invalid non-alphabetic values, malformed email formats are accepted, length limits are inconsistent, and required-field validation messages are incorrect.
 

Checkout
•  Below Medium
Thirteen defects affect checkout and Book Information. Multiple fields accept invalid values, the news subscription checkbox is selected by default, invalid email formats are accepted, and valid contact information leads to a 404 page.
 

Portrait Gallery

Clients Gallery
